This is a healthy wound.
Intervention:

  • 1. Take pictures with Rover and chart on EPIC.
  • 2. 2 RNs Co-sign.
  • 3. Nursing driven to consult a wound care nurse for clean wound assessment, dressing recommendations for all HAPI wounds, surgical wounds and bed recommendations.
  • 4. Nursing driven to consult for poor nutrition intake; unplanned weight loss; failure of wound granulation; low albumin and prealbumin.

For Deep Dry Wound dressing material to hydrate and maintain moisture:

  • Filler dressing option: NS-moist gauze packing; Vashe-moist gauze packing
  • Cover dressing option: Gauze + transparent/waterproof adhesive dressing: Tegaderm; Polyskin

For Shallow Dry Wound dressing material to hydrate and maintain moisture:

  • Hydrogels: Medihoney gel; Woun’dres Collagen; Silver gel (for infected wound); Plurogel; Kerralite cool border
  • Hydrocolloids: Medihoney Hydrocolloids; DuoDERM Extra Thin CGF; ComFeel
  • Non-adherent Impregnated: Curity Oil emulsion gauze; Vaseline gauze; Xeroform gauze; Cutimed Sorbact gel
  • Non-impregnated: Telfa; Mepore (tape border)
  • Secures dressings with Transparent adhesive dressing: Tegaderm; Polyskin
  • Adhesive tape: Medipore; MedFix; Silicon Tape
  • Foam: Mepilex

This is an infected wound with pus.
Intervention:

  • 1. Take pictures with Rover and chart on EPIC.
  • 2. 2 RNs Co-sign.
  • 3. Notify MD who will consult PTWC, ID team for debridement, pulsed lavage, NPWT (negative pressure wound therapy, Compression therapy, VeraFlo system or Biological debriders( Maggots).
  • 4. Request wound culture order from MD.
  • 5. Nursing driven to consult for poor nutrition intake; unplanned weight loss; failure of wound granulation; low albumin and prealbumin.

Correct technique for swab culture:

  • 1. Swab only viable tissue.
  • 2. Do not culture pus, slough or eschar.
  • 3. Flush the wound with sterile saline.
  • 4. Moisture the swab with NS to improve bacterial adherence.
  • 5. Forcefully swab 1sq cm of viable tissue for 5 seconds to express fluid from the underlying wound.
  • 6. Send lab.

    • For Deep Wet Wound dressing material to absorb drainage and need absorptive filter:

    • Filler dressing option: Hydrofiber: Aquacel rope; Alginate (flat or rope); Aquacel Ag (for infected wound); Plain packing strips (¼,½,1-inch); Iodoform packing strips (¼,½,1-inch)
    • Rolled Gauze (Woven): Kerlix roll
    • Mesalt (for autolytic debridement)

    For Shallow Wet Wound dressing material to absorb drainage and need absorptive filter:

    • Foam: Polymem; Mepilex (4x4; heel; sacral; Ag)
    • Abd pads Calcium Alginate: Medihoney Alginate
    • Hydrofiber: Aquacel; Aquacel Extra; Aquacel Ag (infected wound)
    • Hydrocolloid: Exuderm (4x4)
    • Very absorptive: Sofsorb; Drawtex; Mextra
    • Contact layer: Mepitel One; Cutimed Sorbact
